Fish-Fry

Description :

Overall, pan-frying is considered healthier than deep-frying due to the smaller amounts of oil it uses. Additionally, it's best to choose an oil that's stable at high heat and will add healthier fats to your fish. Olive oil is one healthy option.

Ingredients

  • Fishes
  • oil and 2 eggs
  • Salt & Masala
  • Water
  • smusterd powder

Instructions

  1. Pour 2-inches of vegetable oil into a large skillet.
  2. Place skillet over large burner and heat oil over medium high heat.
  3. To check if the oil is hot enough, drop in 1-inch cube of white bread.
  4. The bread should brown in a 40 count.
