Prawns-Biryani

Description :

The easiest and quickest Biryani recipe, that you can cook in about 40 minutes! Prawns take very less time to marinate in comparison with chicken or meat, thus reducing the preparation time. Prawns cooked in yogurt, spices, onions and layered with rice, makes this dish bursting with flavours and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • Prawns
  • oil and 2 eggs
  • Salt & Masala
  • Water
  • Cooked Rice

Instructions

  1. Mix the turmeric, chilli powder, salt, ginger, garlic, onion, garam masala and 1/2 cup yogurt and marinate the prawns in it,
  2. Then heat the oil and saute the prawn mixture in it over high heat, till brown.
  3. Take off the heat and mix in the rest of the yogurt, cream, coriander and half the fried onions
  4. Add the prawn mixture in a layer over this and cover with the rest of the rice
